FAQ for Startups

Common questions from early-stage businesses about marketing and working with us.

We're bootstrapped. Can we afford professional marketing? +

Absolutely! We specialize in working with startups and small businesses on limited budgets. Our packages start at just $99 (Email Marketing Setup), and we offer pay-as-you-grow options. Many of our clients start with basic services and scale up as their business grows. Plus, new clients get 10% discount on their first package!

How quickly can we expect to see results? +

Results vary by service. For PPC advertising, you can see initial results within 1-2 weeks. SEO typically takes 3-4 months to show significant improvements. Social media growth is often visible within the first month. We provide monthly reports so you can track progress from day one. For startups, we focus on quick wins that provide immediate value while building long-term strategies.

Do we need to sign a long-term contract? +

No long-term contracts required. We work on a month-to-month basis with a 30-day notice period. We believe our services should provide enough value that you want to continue working with us, not because you're locked into a contract. The only exception is our Startup Launch Package which is a one-time project fee.

What if we have zero marketing experience? +

That's perfectly fine! Many of our clients come to us with no marketing background. We provide education along with implementation. Our Monthly Marketing Coaching package is specifically designed for founders who want to learn while we handle the technical aspects. We explain everything in plain English, not marketing jargon.

Can you help if we don't have a website yet? +

Yes, we can help with that too! Our Startup Launch Package includes basic website setup. If you prefer to build it yourself, we can recommend user-friendly platforms and provide guidance. We can also help you establish a presence on social media and other channels while your website is being developed.

Still have questions? We're here to help startups succeed.
